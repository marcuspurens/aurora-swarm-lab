# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful and professional for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on ideas, code, and outcomes.
- Accept feedback and discuss tradeoffs in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- Repeated disruptive behavior in issues, PRs, or discussions

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this policy.
Serious or repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.
